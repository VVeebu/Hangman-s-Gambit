Monopoly:The exclusive possession or control of the supply or trade in a commodity or service
Entrepreneurship:The activity of setting up a business or businesses, taking on financial risks in the hope of profit
Domestic:Relating to activities, affairs, or products within a particular country
Venture Capital:A form of private equity financing provided by investors to startup companies and small businesses with high growth potential
Derivatives:Financial contracts whose value is dependent on the value of an underlying asset
Econometrics:The application of statistical methods to economic data
Hyperinflation:Extremely rapid or out of control inflation
Antitrust:Relating to legislation preventing or controlling trusts or other monopolies
Microeconomics:The part of economics concerned with single factors and the effects of individual decisions
Macroeconomics:The part of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors
