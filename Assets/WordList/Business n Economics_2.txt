Segmentation:Way of aggregating prospective buyers into groups with common needs and who respond similarly to a marketing action
Supply:Is the amount of a resource that firms, producers, labourers, providers of financial assets, or other economic agents
Fiscal: Relating to government revenue, taxation, and public spending
Demand:The desire and ability of consumers to purchase goods or services at different price levels
Equity:The value of the shares issued by a company
Asset:Any resource owned by an individual or organization that has economic value and can provide future benefits
Budget:An estimate of income and expenditure for a set period of time
Income:Money received by an individual or business from various sources
Stock:Shares of ownership in a corporation that represent a claim on part of the company's assets and earnings
Revenue:The total income generated by a business from its normal operations, such as sales of goods and services, before deducting expenses
